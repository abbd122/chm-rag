ZW_API_C ezwErrors ZwCSYSCreateByThreePointsInit ( szwDatumCSYSMethodThreePoints *  threePoints)

Initializes the parameters of the ZwCSYSCreateByThreePoints(). threePoints.origin is the origin. threePoints.xPoint is the point on the X-axis. threePoints.yPoint is the Y-axis projection point.
