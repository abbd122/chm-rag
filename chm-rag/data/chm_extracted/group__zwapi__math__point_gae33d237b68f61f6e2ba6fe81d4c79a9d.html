ZwPreEdgePointDistanceGet

Acquire the minimum distance between the point and the curve on face, the difference
from cvxPntEntDist() is that the function will be output the closest point on the UV line
of the face.

[in]idPreEdgeindex of pre-edge
[out]Tparameter value at closest point (NULL to ignore)
[out]TargetPntcoordinates of closest point (NULL to ignore)
