◆ ZwSketchListGet()

ZW_API_C ezwErrors ZwSketchListGet ( int *  countSketch,
szwEntityHandle **  sketchList 
)

#include <zwapi_sketch_general.h>

Outputs a list of database id's of sketches in the active part's history in the order they are listed there.If there are no sketches, "*countSketch" is output as zero and "*sketchList" as NULL.

Note
The caller is responsible to free the "sketchList" by using ZwEntityHandleListFree.
Parameters
[out]countSketchnumber of sketches in the active part
[out]sketchListlist of id's of sketches in the active part history
